About

FLOWERS always worked with suppliers directly, so we can guarantee the high quality of our goods. Satisfying the needs of our customers, we hope to get a positive experience. In our stores you can find beautiful solutions for your needs.

We partner with local florists to hand-craft floral arrangements available for same-day. In addition to delivering flowers, we support locally-owned retail florists by providing technology, marketing, and digital services to members of our florist network.
